Google LSAs

Get the Top Spot on Google Search Result Pages

Google Local Services Ads (LSAs) are now available to law firms nationwide. Google LSAs are sponsored listings that appear at the very top of a search engine result page (SERP) - even above Google’s PPC ads. The ads display a green checkmark stating GOOGLE SCREENED, and include the law firm’s Google My Business (GMB) review rating. They are available for the following practice areas:

Bankruptcy, Business Law, Contract Law, Criminal Law, Disability Law, DUI, Estate Planning, Family Law, Immigration, Intellectual Property, Labor Law, Litigation, Malpractice, Personal Injury, Real Estate, Tax, and Traffic.

Legal Industry News

New Formal Ethics Opinion Gives Additional Guidance on Harassment and Discrimination

The ABA has issued a new opinion regarding Model Rule 8.4(g), which bars discrimination and harassment in legal practice on the basis of race, religion, sex, and other characteristics.

Legal Industry Adds Jobs For a Second Month After Huge April Loss

Following a significant drop this spring, legal jobs have rebounded in May and June, though the overall number of legal industry jobs in June was less than it was last year at that time.

Another State Moves Closer to Mandating Tech CLE, But Limited to Cybersecurity

New York could join Florida and North Carolina as the third US state to require lawyers to take continuing legal education related to technology, though it would pertain only to cybersecurity.

LGBTQ+ Lawyers and Attorneys With Disabilities Report Prevalent Discrimination, ABA Study Says
