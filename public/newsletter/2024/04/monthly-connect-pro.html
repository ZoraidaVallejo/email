Juries that lack diversity not only sideline and neglect communities of color but also lead to unjust convictions and inequitable sentences, unfairly impacting Black individuals and people of color. In this presentation by Angie Setzer, she highlighted the significance of diverse juries, delved into the historical context of racial bias in jury selection, and explored tactics to address and eradicate illegal racial discrimination during jury selection.
